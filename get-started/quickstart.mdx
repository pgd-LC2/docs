## Build your first website

Get started with AISiteBuilder by creating, customizing, and publishing your first website.

### Step 1: Create your account

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign up">
Visit [aisitebuilder.com](https://aisitebuilder.com) and create your account using email or social login.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose your plan">
Select a plan that fits your needs. Start with the free tier to explore the platform.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Start building

<Steps>
<Step title="Describe your website">
In the chat interface, describe the website you want to build. Be specific about:
- Purpose and goals
- Key features needed
- Design preferences
- Target audience

Example: "Create a landing page for a fitness app with a hero section, features list, pricing table, and contact form. Use a modern, energetic design with blue and orange colors."
</Step>

<Step title="Review the plan">
AI will create a detailed plan for your website. Review it and make any adjustments before building.
</Step>

<Step title="Build">
Click "Build" and watch as AI creates your website in real-time. You'll see the preview update as components are added.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Customize

<Steps>
<Step title="Make changes">
Use the chat to request changes:
- "Change the hero background to a gradient"
- "Add a testimonials section"
- "Update the contact form to include a phone number field"
</Step>

<Step title="Use the visual editor">
Click on elements in the preview to edit them directly. Adjust text, images, colors, and spacing.
</Step>

<Step title="Test responsiveness">
Switch between desktop, tablet, and mobile views to ensure your site looks great on all devices.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Step 4: Publish

<Steps>
<Step title="Configure settings">
Set up your website settings:
- Choose a domain name
- Configure SEO settings
- Set up analytics
</Step>

<Step title="Deploy">
Click "Publish" to make your website live. Your site will be deployed instantly with SSL certificate.
</Step>

<Step title="Share">
Your website is now live! Share the URL with the world.
</Step>
</Steps>
