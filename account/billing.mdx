## Managing your subscription

### Upgrading

Upgrade your plan anytime:

1. Go to **Account Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm upgrade

Your new plan activates immediately.

### Downgrading

Downgrade at the end of your billing period:

1. Go to **Account Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select lower tier
4. Confirm downgrade

Changes take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

<Warning>
  Downgrading may limit access to features. Ensure your usage fits within the new plan limits.
</Warning>

### Canceling

Cancel your subscription anytime:

1. Go to **Account Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Provide feedback (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

## Billing

### Payment methods

Accepted payment methods:

- **Credit cards** - Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Debit cards**
- **PayPal**
- **Bank transfer** (Enterprise only)

### Updating payment method

Change your payment information:

1. Go to **Account Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Payment Method**
3. Add new payment method
4. Set as default
5. Remove old method (optional)

### Billing cycle

Choose your billing frequency:

- **Monthly** - Billed every month
- **Annual** - Billed once per year (save 17%)

### Invoices

Access your billing history:

1. Go to **Account Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Invoices**
3. View or download invoices
4. Email invoices to accounting

## Billing issues

### Failed payment

If a payment fails:

1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Update payment method
3. Retry payment manually
4. Or wait for automatic retry (3 attempts)

After 3 failed attempts, your account may be suspended.

### Refunds

Refund policy:

- **30-day money-back guarantee** for new subscriptions
- **Pro-rated refunds** for annual plans (first 30 days)
- **No refunds** for token purchases
- **No refunds** after 30 days

Request refund:

1. Contact support
2. Provide reason
3. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

### Billing disputes

Dispute a charge:

1. Contact support with invoice number
2. Explain the issue
3. Provide supporting information
4. Resolution within 3 business days

### Tax invoices

Download tax invoices:

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
    Yes, you can upgrade immediately or downgrade at the end of your billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens to my websites if I cancel?">
    Your websites remain published but you can't edit them. Upgrade again to regain access.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do unused tokens roll over?">
    Purchased token packs roll over indefinitely. Monthly plan tokens reset each month.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Yes, within 30 days of your initial subscription. See refund policy above.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get an invoice?">
    Invoices are automatically emailed after each payment. Access them in Account Settings → Billing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pause my subscription?">
    No, but you can cancel and resubscribe later. Your data is retained for 90 days.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
